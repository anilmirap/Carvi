# Carvi

**Tagline:** “Özel serviste yeni standart.”

Carvi is a premium digital service ecosystem and operating system for the independent automotive service market.

It is designed for two connected audiences:
- **Independent expert workshops** that need strong operational tools.
- **Car owners** who need trusted, transparent, and fast access to service.

Carvi is **not** a generic garage app, a simple repair marketplace, or a basic appointment tool. It is a **trust layer + operating system** for workshop operations and customer confidence.

## Product Direction

### Core Thesis
Independent workshops deliver high expertise but often run on fragmented tools, manual processes, and inconsistent records. This creates cost, delay, and trust problems.

Carvi standardizes critical workflows end-to-end:
- Maintenance and repair operations
- Customer and vehicle records
- Spare parts and service line documentation
- Quote and approval transparency
- Warranty and service history traceability

### MVP Priority
The first release focuses on the **workshop operations web app**.

Customer mobile experience will come after operational foundations are stable.
