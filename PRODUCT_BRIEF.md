# PRODUCT_BRIEF

## 1) Product Name
**Carvi**

## 2) Brand Tagline
**“Özel serviste yeni standart.”**

## 3) One-Sentence Positioning
Carvi is a premium trust layer and operating system for independent automotive service workshops, helping them run service operations digitally while giving car owners transparent and reliable service access.

## 4) Problem Statement
Independent workshops are often highly capable technically but operationally fragmented.

Common pain points:
- Customer and vehicle data spread across notebooks, chat, spreadsheets, or disconnected tools
- Work orders and service details are inconsistently recorded
- Spare parts usage and stock movement are hard to track
- Quote approvals are slow or verbally managed, causing disputes
- Service history is incomplete, reducing trust and repeat business
- Owners struggle to compare and trust service quality and process clarity

Result:
- Lower efficiency
- Operational risk
- Revenue leakage
- Reduced customer confidence

## 5) Target Users

### Primary (MVP)
1. **Independent workshop owners/managers**
2. **Service advisors/front-desk operators**
3. **Technicians (limited role-based interaction)**

### Secondary (Post-MVP)
4. **Car owners** via customer-facing mobile app

## 6) Core Value Proposition

### For Workshops
- Standardized service operations from intake to delivery
- Faster, clearer quote and approval process
- Better control over service/parts records and stock visibility
- Reliable service history for every vehicle
- Stronger customer trust and retention

### For Car Owners
- Faster access to trusted independent workshops
- Transparent quote and approval experience
- Clear historical record of services and parts used

## 7) What Carvi Is Not
- Not a generic appointment-only product
- Not a low-context marketplace that only matches users to shops
- Not a lightweight invoicing add-on
- Not a template garage management clone

Carvi’s differentiation is trust, traceability, and operational depth.

## 8) Product Principles
1. **Trust by design** — Every action should improve clarity and accountability.
2. **Operational completeness** — Core workshop workflows must connect end-to-end.
3. **Role-aware simplicity** — Simple interfaces without reducing operational rigor.
4. **Evidence-first records** — Service decisions and approvals must be auditable.
5. **Pragmatic compliance** — Data and workflow decisions should be regulation-aware.

## 9) MVP Product Surface
MVP starts with a **web application for workshop operations**.

In scope at launch:
- Workshop onboarding
- User roles
- Customer management
- Vehicle management
- Work order management
- Service line items
- Spare parts line items
- Quote approval workflow
- Service history
- Basic dashboard

## 10) Success Indicators (Early)
- Workshops onboarded and activated
- Work orders created per active workshop
- Quote approval turnaround time
- Percentage of jobs with complete service + parts records
- Repeat vehicle service rate
- Customer dispute rate reduction (qualitative + quantitative)

## 11) Tone & Experience Direction
Carvi experience should feel:
- Professional and high-trust
- Clear and structured
- Detail-rich without clutter
- Premium but practical
